Spanish Beans and Rice (Arroz Con Frijoles)

Abuela's Beans and Rice

This hearty bowl of black beans and seasoned rice will make grandma proud!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups White Rice
  • 15oz Black Beans
  • 1 Yellow Onion (small)
  • 2 tsp Adobo by Goya* seasoning
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Crushed Black Pepper
  • 2 packets Sazon Goya* with Saffron

Abuela's Beans and Rice

This hearty bowl of black beans and seasoned rice will make grandma proud!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups White Rice
  • 15oz Black Beans
  • 1 Yellow Onion (small)
  • 2 tsp Minced Garlic
  • 2 tsp Adobo by Goya* seasoning
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Crushed Black Pepper
  • 2 packets Sazon Goya* with Saffron
  • 1 tbsp Canola Oil
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Make rice according to package directions. Chop and Sautee onion in oil and butter. When onions soften, add garlic.
  2. Rinse beans and add to pan. Heat through.
  3. Mix in rice. Add seasonings and fry for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and serve warm.
